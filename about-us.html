About Us

Who we are?

SMART IMPORTS AND EXPORTS is a quality and excellence driven import/export company with a vision to provide the world access to best quality Indian rice, fresh fruits, fresh vegetables and spices with world class quality, infrastructural and reasonable price along with vast clientele we strive to bridge our customers faster than others.

Let’s see how do we do it.

We have a well trained native team who have been living in the roots of manufacturing hub. We first understand your product needs and find the right factory from our extensive research and database and then we filter them with our proprietary lens based on your product needs. We provide insight details of suppliers with oversight to protect you against fraud and quality risks that most importer/exporter face these days. With our expertise we are able to find not only large factories but also the smallest one which can fulfill your needs. We make your risk nearly zero by deploying quality control at every major step in production line when required. Our turn key factory project can help you inside out from deployment cost of project to deriving the selling cost of the product making it complete turnkey. We deploy the right team to help you start your own production house in any part of the india.

If you already have a supplier and want us to handle the end to end solution, just sent us their quote or proforma invoice and packing list we will send you a free quote to your email.

    For us to give you an accurate quote Please sent us the following information:

  • 1. Value of order
  • 2. CBM or size of the boxes
  • 3. Weight of total order
  • 4. HSN code
  • 5. Product description
  • 6. Mode: Air/Sea

    • You can also share the Proforma invoice and packing list if you have.
